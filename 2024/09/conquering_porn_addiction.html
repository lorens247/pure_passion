Conquering Porn addiction

Published: 24th Feruary, 2024

Pornography has become like a deadly pandemic in our day. Every Christian needs to be on guard and in order to stay safe, practice Job 31:1 - “I made a covenant with my eyes not to look lustfully at a woman."

Dangers of porn

  1. It causes men to view women as mere sex objects

    Porn will make you see a woman as an object (thing) to be used, rather than a person to be loved (adored). Porn addicts very easily abuse women physically and sexually.

  2. It creates guilt that is hard to shake off

    Porn makes one feel guilty, ashamed and disgusted with himself. It often leads to one having an inferiority complex, lack of confidence, loss of memory, depression and even suicidal tendencies.

  3. It encourages one to live a secret double life

    Porn, like most sexual sins, is mostly done in secret. It pushes one to become secluded and secretive; often lying and scheming to avoid getting caught. This has broken many marriages and cost others their jobs and reputations.

  4. It forms memories that hinder pure relationships

    Porn will make it difficult to think purely of the opposite sex. Some people can't stay without sex, because porn has messed up their minds.

  5. It grieves the Holy Spirit

    Porn quenches the Spirit and opens up one's life to demons; destroys destinies and can ultimately send one to hell, if he/she does not repent - Galatians 5:19-21.

Why people engage in porn

  1. Curiosity

    The average Christian's mind is very curious and hence when we see posts on Facebook like, "naked pictures of 'so and so' celebrity found", our curious minds often want to check to be sure if it is true. This is how many Christians get addicted.

  2. Boredom

    We often say the idle mind is the devil's workshop. Sadly, many Christians are very idle. Nothing awakens the flesh like idleness and boredom (Exo. 32:1-8).

  3. Depression

    Some Christians are daily faced with the reality of their failures - at work, business, marriage etc. When depression sets in, the mind begins to crave excitement and porn becomes a way to “get high” and escape reality.

Breaking free from porn

  1. Repent

    Resolve to turn away from porn. One proof that you have truly repented is that you will hate the sin you once loved. You can hate porn by focusing on God’s love and judgement (Rom.11:22). Declare boldly: I hate porn and I refuse to be a slave to it in Jesus Name.

  2. Starve your flesh (Rom.13:14)

    Deliberately put off your phone or anything that awakens the urge for porn. You have to be very serious about turning away from porn (1Corinthians 9:27).

  3. Get drunk in the Spirit

    By praying hard and long in tongues (1Cor. 14:4) and by meditating on God's Word (Psalm 119:11). Nobody gets drunk by taking sips, so you need to drink a lot from God's Word and Spirit to stay full of the Holy Spirit and keep your flesh subdued - Ephesians 5:18.

  4. Resist the devil (James 4:7)

    There are spirits behind porn addiction. You must confront and conquer them. You have authority in Christ to bind the activities of the devil - Luke 10:19. Command the spirits behind porn to loose their hold on you and be gone from you in Jesus Name (Matt.18:18).

  5. Be accountable

  6. Talk to your Pastor. The sin you expose, becomes easier to resist. Also request for prayers andcounselling (Prov. 28:13).
