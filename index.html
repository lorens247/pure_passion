Pure Passion Project

Pure passion was created to help solve the basic challenges many teenagers and young adults face in this generation.

Our Focus

The vision is renewing the youthful mind, and to achieve this, the project focuses on 3 aspect of every teenager & young adult's life: PURPOSE, PASSION, PURITY

Purpose

We help teenagers to discover their purpose in life, inspiring them to pursue it in the light of God's word.

Passion

We help them cultivate a passion and hunger for the things of God. For example; prayer, studying God's word, and evangelism.

Purity

We help them to understand what it means to stay sexually pure in this world.
